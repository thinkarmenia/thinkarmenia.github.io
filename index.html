Family First

To be there for our brothers and sisters

Welcome Home

There is no place like home. That's why its our job to be there for our family.

If you've seen the recent advancements taking place in Armenia, it is no question that times are changing and we should support it as much as we can!

Future of Armenia

Investing in the youth has never been so critical. Our program will aim to provide mentoring and support in personal, social, and financial parts of life. Helping these kids become the people they dream to be is our main obligation.

Gift of Life

Anyone given the chance can have limitless opportunities, but unfortunately not many people have the same luxuries as we do. A small act of kindness can change the world for someone so we strive to provide as much as possible.

Our Mission

Mentoring

Our main goal is to show how the world is changing through technology and help them get started on that path. From creating businesses and sharing ideas, to showcasing art and personal projects, we want to guide them in the new opportunities present in technology.

Donations

Electronics, school supplies, clothing, and money will help us provide the basic needs for the kids. (Money is used to buy brand new wholesale goods, and also for helping people with financial burdens.)

Armenian Experience

Social media allows people to conect and through the Think Armenia network we are continually connecting the Armenian community. You can see all of our projects on our media pages and directly communicate and share ideas with others!

Volunteer Opportunities

I started this website after learning how small the world is through technology, we have direct access to helping others out. If you want to join our mission, we have something for everyone - message us!
